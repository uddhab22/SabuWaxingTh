Frequently Asked Questions

For best results, exfoliate the area 24 hours before your appointment and avoid moisturizers on the day. Hair should be at least 1/4 inch long (about the length of a grain of rice). Avoid sun exposure and tanning 24 hours before and after your appointment.

Sugaring uses a natural paste made of sugar, lemon, and water that's applied at body temperature and removes hair in the direction of growth. It's less painful than waxing and better for sensitive skin. Waxing uses resin-based wax that's applied warm and removed against the direction of hair growth.

For most skin types, we recommend a facial every 4-6 weeks to maintain optimal skin health. This aligns with the skin's natural renewal cycle. However, we can customize a schedule based on your specific skin concerns and goals during your consultation.
